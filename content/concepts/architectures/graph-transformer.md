# Graph Transformers

Graph transformers combine graph-structured inputs with attention-based message mixing. They are a bridge between [[concepts/architectures/gnn|GNNs]] and [[concepts/architectures/transformer|Transformers]].

## Uses

- Molecular and protein graphs with long-range interactions.
- Pairwise relation modeling over atoms, residues, or contacts.
- Geometry-aware variants connected to [[concepts/geometric-deep-learning/index|geometric deep learning]].
