# JEPA

Joint-embedding predictive architectures train models to predict representations of missing or future parts of data in embedding space.

## Why It Matters

- Avoids reconstructing every low-level detail.
- Focuses learning on useful abstractions.
- Can be considered for sequences, structures, graphs, and multimodal settings.
