# Contrastive Learning

Contrastive learning trains representations by pulling related views together and pushing unrelated views apart.

## Design Questions

- What defines a positive pair?
- What defines a negative pair?
- Which augmentations preserve the meaning needed for downstream tasks?
- Does the split avoid leakage through near-duplicates or shared families?
