# Research Question

A research question states what unknown a run, paper review, or analysis is meant to reduce. A vague topic is not yet a research question.

Weak:

$$
\text{Does method A work?}
$$

Stronger:

$$
\text{Does method A improve metric } M \text{ on split } S
\text{ compared with baseline } B?
$$

## Good Questions

- Name the task and metric.
- Name the data split or benchmark.
- Name the baseline or prior belief.
- State the expected mechanism.
- State what would change the next decision.

## Checks

- Can the question be answered by a concrete observation?
- Is the scope small enough for one experiment or paper note?
- Does it avoid bundling multiple questions?
- Is the question public-safe, without internal project names or unpublished claims?
