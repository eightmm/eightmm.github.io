# Structure-Based Modeling Papers

Structure-based modeling paper note는 docking, conformer, pose generation, scoring, virtual screening, molecular generation, protein-ligand evaluation, structure-aware learning을 다룹니다.

## Reading Axes

- What object is modeled: ligand, pocket, complex, pose, affinity, or ranking?
- Is the method search-based, generative, discriminative, geometric, or hybrid?
- Are pose quality, binding affinity, and virtual screening metrics separated?
- Are ligand scaffold split, protein-family split, and geometry validity checked?
- Are negative set construction, activity cliffs, assay harmonization, and applicability domain discussed?
- Does the paper separate pose quality, affinity prediction, and screening enrichment claims?
